# GhostLock

**GhostLock** is a simple, ultra-lightweight desktop app for Windows that locks multiple files into a single secure file (`.dve`). It acts as a privacy shield, preventing cloud drives, AI crawlers, and hackers from reading your personal files.

---

## Why use GhostLock?

Most major cloud storage providers (like Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox) scan and read your uploaded files to train AI models and index search terms. This means your private photos, documents, and spreadsheets are actively read on their servers.

**GhostLock** solves this privacy issue. It scrambles (encrypts) your files on your computer *before* you upload them. The cloud drives only see a locked, unreadable file. This shields your data from cloud scanners, AI engines, and hackers.

---

## Key Features

- **Double-Layer Lock**: Automatically locks each file individually, groups them together, and locks the whole package for maximum security.
- **100% Offline**: Runs entirely on your computer without connecting to the internet. Your passwords and files never leave your machine.
- **No Installation Needed**: It is a tiny, single file (`ghostlock.exe`) that runs instantly.
- **Drag & Drop**: Easily select files by dragging them straight from your folder into the app.

---

## How to Use

### To Lock (Encrypt) Files:
1. Open `ghostlock.exe`.
2. Drag and drop the files you want to secure into the app (or click **Add Files...**).
3. Type a secure password.
4. Click **Encrypt to DVE File** and select where to save your locked file.

### To Unlock (Decrypt) Files:
1. Go to the **Decrypt DVE File** tab.
2. Select your locked `.dve` file.
3. Type your password.
4. Click **Decrypt & Extract Files** and select a folder to restore your files.
